My Favorite Places in San Diego

I spent four years living in San Diego. Here are (some of) my favorite places in this amazing city.

The University of San Diego

My alma mater, and one of America's most beautiful college campuses.

Balboa Park

Home to several museums, gardens, and the world-famous San Diego Zoo, Balboa Park is a must-visit for anyone looking to see the very best of San Diego.

Gaslamp Quarter

Located in downtown San Diego, the Gaslamp Quarter is perfect for a night out with friends.

San Diego Central Library

A fairly new addition to San Diego, this library is great.
